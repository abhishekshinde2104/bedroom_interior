Bedroom · 15 m² · Turn 1

10 laminate palettes — bright, fresh, minimal

Each card maps laminates to the five surfaces: closet, bed-back wall panel, study table, loft cabinet, and the base wall tone. All palettes assume matte laminates with one textured or soft-gloss accent. Schematic shows the bed wall elevation.

1a
Nordic Dawn
Closet
Frosty white matte, handle-less
Bed wall + loft
Light oak woodgrain laminate
Study table
Pure white with oak edge band
Accent
Sage green on one loft shutter
The safest bright-minimal combo: oak warmth keeps white from feeling clinical; sage adds freshness without noise.
1b
Cloud & Ash
Closet
Snow white super-matte
Bed wall + loft
Grey-washed ash woodgrain
Study table
White top, ash legs/gable
Accent
Charcoal matte study-table drawer face
Cool, gallery-like calm. Best if your light is warm (south/west windows) — the greys stay crisp, never cold.
1c
Fresh Linen
Closet + loft
Linen-textured off-white laminate
Bed wall
Powder blue matte panel
Study table
Warm white, pale-sand edge
Accent
Pale sand on desk-side shelf
Airy and breezy — the blue bed wall is the single statement; everything else recedes into textured whites.
1d
Terra Soft
Closet
Ivory matte, slim groove handles
Bed wall
Muted terracotta fluted panel
Study + loft
Pale beech woodgrain
Accent
Terracotta repeats on one loft door
Warm and lively — terracotta gives the “happening” energy while ivory + beech keep it 80% minimal.
1e
Olive Grove
Closet
Deep olive matte — the hero surface
Bed wall
Warm white slatted panel
Study + loft
Honey oak woodgrain
Accent
Brass-tone handles (hardware, not laminate)
Flips the formula: the closet carries the color, the bed wall stays quiet. Grounded, botanical, still bright.
1f
Morning Mist
Closet + loft
Mist grey super-matte
Bed wall
Dusty aqua panel behind headboard
Study table
White top with pale-oak apron
Accent
Pale oak open niche in closet run
Spa-like and serene. Aqua reads fresh without being loud; greys do the minimalist work.
1g
Butter & Bone
Closet
Bone white matte
Bed wall
Pale sand woodgrain
Study table
White with butter-yellow drawer faces
Loft
Butter yellow soft-gloss
Sunniest of the ten — yellow lives up high on the loft so it lifts the room without dominating eye level.
1h
Slate Minimal
Closet + loft
Warm white matte, continuous grain
Bed wall
Slate blue matte panel
Study table
Natural oak top + white body
Accent
Oak floating shelf on slate wall
The most “adult” option — deep but not dark, slate anchors sleep while whites keep daytime brightness.
1i
Blush Calm
Closet + loft
Blush-tinted off-white matte
Bed wall
Dusty rose fluted panel
Study table
Warm white + light walnut top
Accent
Light walnut vertical strip beside closet
Soft-romantic minimalism — rose tones are muted enough to feel warm rather than sweet.
1j
Monochrome Warm
Closet + loft
Greige matte, one continuous plane
Bed wall
Warm white micro-groove panel
Study table
White with black matte legs/edge
Accent
Black matte details only — handles, desk edge
Strictest minimalism of the set. No color — contrast and texture carry the interest. Ages best.
